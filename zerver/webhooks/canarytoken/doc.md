# Zulip Thinkst Canarytoken integration

See your Thinkst Canarytoken alerts in Zulip!

This integration works with Canarytokens from
[canarytokens.org][canarytokens], not Thinkst's paid product. See the
[Zulip Thinkst integration](/integrations/doc/thinkst) for those!

{start_tabs}

1. {!create-channel.md!}

1. {!create-an-incoming-webhook.md!}

1. {!generate-webhook-url-basic.md!}

1. Go to [canarytokens.org][canarytokens], and select the type of
   webhook that you want to create.

1. Add a reminder note.

1. In the **Provide an email address of webhook URL** field, enter the
   generated URL above.

1. Finally, select **Create my canarytoken**.

{end_tabs}
